Why Smart Cycle?

01

Smart Lock Powered by ESP32

Our cycles are equipped with an ESP32-based electronic lock that ensures secure and controlled access. The lock only opens after successful booking and payment, and automatically locks again when your ride time expires—no manual intervention needed.

02

Book in Seconds

With a simple web interface, users can choose their ride duration, see the cost instantly, and complete the booking in just a few clicks. The system handles everything—from time tracking to access control—seamlessly.

03

Pay Only for What You Use

Every ride is time-controlled. Once your selected duration ends, the system automatically updates the lock status, ensuring fair usage and better availability for everyone.

04

Designed for Modern Mobility

The Smart Cycle Rental System is ideal for campuses, corporate parks, and smart cities. It reduces operational costs, removes the need for manual supervision, and provides a scalable solution for shared mobility.
